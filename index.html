Our History

Rice Cakes was born out of a simple idea: to create a space where cakes meets community and creativity.Founded in 2024, our journey began with a passion for crafting quality cakes and offering a personalized experience that caters to every palate.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a vibrant hub for rice cakes lovers, pet owners, and students alike, blending the art of cake-making with a welcoming atmosphere where everyone can feel at home Whether you're grabbing a quick cup on the go or settling in for a study session, Rice Cakes is here to fuel your journey, one cup at a time.
